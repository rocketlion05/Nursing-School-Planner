Frequently asked questions

How does the HESI score calculator work?

The planner compares your inputs with program-level GPA, entrance exam, prerequisite, seat, and applicant estimates to produce a planning range.

Is the estimate a guarantee?

No. It is a planning estimate only. Schools make final admissions decisions based on their own policies and applicant pools.

How can I improve my estimate?

Raise your GPA or science GPA, complete prerequisites, prepare for entrance exams, document experience, and build a balanced school list.
