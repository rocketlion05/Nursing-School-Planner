Frequently asked questions

Nursing school admissions FAQ

Quick answers for pre-nursing students comparing GPA, TEAS scores, prerequisites, and application timing.

How does the nursing school odds calculator work?

The planner compares your GPA, science GPA, TEAS or HESI score, completed prerequisites, experience, and readiness against each program's requirements to create an estimated admissions range.

What GPA do I need for nursing school?

Nursing school GPA expectations vary by program. Review minimum and competitive GPA guidance in the nursing school GPA guide, then use the planner to compare your profile.

What TEAS score do nursing schools want?

TEAS score requirements depend on the school. Some programs use a minimum score, while others rank applicants by test score, GPA, prerequisites, and experience. Start with the TEAS score requirements guide.

How can I improve my nursing application plan?

Build a balanced list of reach, target, and likely programs, finish prerequisites early, prepare for entrance exams, and track deadlines with the nursing application checklist.

Where should I start if I am new to pre-nursing?

Read how to get into nursing school, then enter your current GPA, test score, prerequisites, and home state in the planner to see which programs fit your profile.

Can I use this as a nursing GPA calculator?

Yes. The GPA planner estimates the future grades you may need for a target GPA, while the admissions calculator compares GPA and science GPA with nursing school requirements.

Support the Creator

Built by a student, for future nursing students

Hi, I’m Nathan — a student at the University of Arkansas and someone who knows how stressful nursing school applications can feel.

When I was applying to the nursing program, I constantly worried about whether I was competitive enough to get in. I spent hours trying to calculate my odds, compare stats, and figure out where I stood. Eventually, I realized I was actually far more competitive than I thought — and that a lot of the stress came from simply not having a clear way to estimate my chances.

That’s why I built this tool.

My goal is to help other students get a realistic idea of where they stand without all the unnecessary panic and uncertainty I went through myself. I hope this calculator gives you a little more clarity, confidence, and peace of mind during the application process.
