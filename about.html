About Nursing School Planner

Nursing School Planner helps pre-nursing students compare requirements, estimate fit, and build a calmer application plan.

Why this site exists

Nursing applications can feel stressful when requirements are scattered across school pages and students do not know whether they are competitive. This site brings GPA planning, entrance exam planning, program comparisons, and application checklists into one place.

The goal is clarity: understand where you stand, decide what to improve, and create a balanced list of reach, target, and likely programs.
