Privacy Policy

This policy explains the basic data practices for Nursing School Planner.

Information stored in your browser

The planner may save your profile inputs, selected programs, and saved program list in your browser's local storage so the dashboard can remember your plan. This data stays on your device unless you clear it or your browser syncs it.

Analytics and external links

The site uses Google Analytics to understand traffic and improve content. External links may lead to Amazon, YouTube, school websites, or Buy Me a Coffee. Those services have their own privacy policies.

No admissions or medical advice

The site provides educational planning estimates only and does not make admissions decisions.
